# **App Name**: Vector: Павлодар

## Core Features:

- Search Functionality: Implement a search bar that allows users to find information about specific streets, notable figures, and cultural phenomena in Павлодар.
- AI-Powered Information Expansion: Provide an input field where users can type questions or requests for more detailed information, with an AI tool providing expanded content based on the search results and user input.
- Street Information Display: Display detailed information about streets in Павлодар, including their history, notable buildings, and current significance.
- Notable Figures Database: Curate and present information about famous individuals associated with Павлодар, with biographies, images, and key achievements.
- Cultural Phenomena Overview: Describe cultural phenomena relevant to Павлодар, such as local traditions, historical events, and artistic movements.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Burnt sienna (#E97451), inspired by the historical and cultural depth of Павлодар, providing warmth and sophistication.
- Background color: Light beige (#F5F5DC), offering a neutral, unobtrusive backdrop to ensure readability and focus on content.
- Accent color: Dusty rose (#D2691E), drawing from analogous shades to highlight interactive elements and important details.
- Body and headline font: 'Literata', a transitional serif offering a literary and slightly vintage feel.
- Use simple, classic icons to represent categories like streets, people, and cultural events.
- Employ a clean, grid-based layout with ample whitespace to enhance readability and navigation.
- Incorporate subtle transitions for search results and AI content to maintain user engagement without being distracting.
